Privacy Policy – Success Journal

Your privacy matters. The Success Journal app was designed to process your entries with as little data usage as possible.

1. Responsible Party

Responsible for this app:
Jürgen Zimolong
Email: juzi7@icloud.com

2. What data is processed in the app?

The app only processes content that you enter yourself, such as journal entries, gratitude notes, reflections, insights, or personal goals.

3. Where is your data stored?

Your data is generally stored locally on your device. If iCloud synchronization is enabled on your Apple device, your content may also be synced across your Apple devices via iCloud.

Synchronization is provided through Apple’s infrastructure. No own external servers are operated to store your content.

4. Apple Services

Apple services may be used for synchronization and certain system functions. Apple’s privacy policies also apply in this context.

5. Tracking, Analytics and Advertising

The app does not use tracking tools, analytics services, or advertising networks. No personal usage behavior is analyzed for advertising purposes.

6. Third Parties

No third-party advertising or analytics SDKs are used. Where technically necessary, only Apple services may be used, for example for iCloud synchronization or in-app purchases.

7. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The app may offer optional paid features or subscriptions. Payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple and the App Store.

We do not receive complete payment details such as credit card numbers. Payment information is processed by Apple under Apple’s own privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable law, you generally have the right to:

  • access your personal data
  • correct inaccurate data
  • delete your data
  • restrict processing
  • object to processing where applicable

Since content is primarily stored locally on your device, you can delete much of your data yourself by removing entries or uninstalling the app.

9. Data Security

The app is designed to process as little data as possible. The security of your data also depends on your device settings and the security mechanisms of the operating system.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if app features or legal requirements change.

11. Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please contact: juzi7@icloud.com
