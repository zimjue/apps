Privacy Policy – Life Wheel

Your privacy is important. The Life Wheel app helps you reflect on different areas of your life and gain clarity.

The app is designed to collect as little personal data as possible. All content is stored locally on your device.

1. What data is processed?

The app stores information that you enter yourself, such as ratings, notes, or reflections inside the app.

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

2. iCloud Synchronization

If iCloud is enabled on your device, your data may optionally be synchronized between your own Apple devices using Apple iCloud services. We do not access, store, or process this data on external servers.

3. Tracking / Analytics / Advertising

The app does not use tracking technologies, analytics services, or advertising frameworks. No personal data is collected for analytics or marketing purposes.

4. Third‑Party Services

The app does not use third‑party services that collect personal data.

5. In‑App Subscription

The app may offer an optional subscription for additional features (for example PDF export). Payments are processed securely through Apple via the App Store.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your data at any time or uninstall the app. Because all data is stored locally on your device, removing the app will also remove the stored data.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact: juzi7@icloud.com
