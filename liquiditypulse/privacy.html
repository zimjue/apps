Privacy Policy – LiquidityPulse

Your privacy matters. The LiquidityPulse app is designed to process as little personal data as possible.

1. Responsible Party

Responsible for this app:
Jürgen Zimmer
Email: juzi7@icloud.com

2. What data is processed in the app?

LiquidityPulse primarily processes public market and macroeconomic data that are loaded to display liquidity, market, and stress signals.

In addition, app settings may be stored locally on your device, such as language preferences, view options, or similar user-side settings.

3. Where does the data come from?

The app retrieves public time series and market data from external sources, in particular through a FRED-related data proxy and through CoinGecko for Bitcoin market data.

4. What technical data may be processed?

When retrieving external data, technically necessary connection data may be processed, such as IP address, timestamps, header information, or device-related network data to the extent required to deliver the requested content.

This processing is carried out by the infrastructure of the respective data providers or related technical services.

5. Where is your data stored?

App settings or locally stored information are generally kept on your device, to the extent the app stores such data at all. No own external servers are operated to store personal user content.

6. Tracking, Analytics and Advertising

The app does not use advertising networks and does not use tracking or analytics SDKs to analyze your personal usage behavior for marketing purposes.

7. Third Parties

External data sources and technical services may be used to provide market data. This includes CoinGecko and the data proxy used for FRED-related series.

These external services are subject to their own privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable law, you generally have the right to:

  • access your personal data
  • correct inaccurate data
  • delete your data
  • restrict processing
  • object to processing where applicable

If app settings are stored locally on your device, you can usually remove them by resetting the app, changing the settings, or uninstalling the app.

9. Data Security

The app is designed to work in a data-minimizing way. The security of your data also depends on your device settings and the security mechanisms of the operating system.

10. Important Notice

LiquidityPulse provides information and historical data but does not constitute investment advice, financial advice, or an individualized recommendation.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if app features, connected services, or legal requirements change.

12. Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please contact: juzi7@icloud.com
